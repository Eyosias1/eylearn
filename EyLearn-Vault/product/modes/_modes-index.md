# Modes Index

## [[active-recall]]

Behaviors: [[confidence-rating]] · [[hint-penalty]] · [[micro-reflection]] · [[weak-spot-requeue]] · [[session-checkpoint]] · [[mistake-review]] · [[ai-grading]] · [[interleaved-practice]] · [[session-timer]]

## [[blurting-mode]]
Topic title only. Write everything you remember. AI highlights gaps.
Behaviors: [[session-timer]]

## [[feynman-mode]]
Explain like a 5th grader. AI rates clarity, completeness, jargon.
Behaviors: [[session-timer]]

## [[pre-test]]
Quiz before studying to prime the brain (hypercorrection effect).
Behaviors: [[interleaved-practice]] · [[session-timer]]

## [[exam-simulation]]
Full exam from course notes + error history. All questions at once, free navigation, one timer, submit in one go. AI grades in batch at the end.
Behaviors: [[ai-grading]] · [[spaced-repetition]] · [[note-ingestion]] · [[micro-reflection]]

## [[worked-examples]]
Study a solved example from your notes, then hide it and reproduce the reasoning chain. AI compares step by step. Only available when notes contain worked examples.
Behaviors: [[ai-grading]] · [[spaced-repetition]]
