# Product Requirements Document (PRD)

---

## Vision

Build a learning app that makes students study **smarter, not harder** — using evidence-based techniques (active recall, spaced repetition, interleaved practice) instead of the passive methods most apps rely on (flashcards, re-reading, highlighting).

---

## The Problem

Most learning apps (Anki, Quizlet, Duolingo) are built around **cued recall** — matching a term to a definition. Research consistently shows this creates an illusion of knowing without producing durable long-term memory.

Students are:
- Studying for hours without retaining information
- Cramming before exams and forgetting everything after
- Using passive methods (highlighting, re-reading) that feel productive but aren't
- Unaware of evidence-based alternatives

---

## The Solution

An app that replaces passive study habits with active, research-backed techniques — and uses AI to handle the **setup and evaluation**, so the user's cognitive effort goes entirely into **retrieval and thinking**.

---

## Target User

| Attribute | Description |
|---|---|
| **Primary** | High school and university students |
| **Secondary** | Self-learners, professionals upskilling |
| **Pain Point** | Studying a lot but not retaining enough |
| **Motivation** | Better grades with less wasted time |
| **Tech Comfort** | Comfortable with mobile and web apps |

---

## Core Principles

1. **The struggle is the learning** — the app never makes it too easy. Difficulty is intentional.
2. **AI sets up and evaluates. The user thinks.** — AI never answers for the user or summarizes content passively.
3. **Evidence over intuition** — every feature maps to a research-backed technique.
4. **Progress is visible** — users can always see how well they know their material and what needs work.

---

## Goals & Success Metrics

| Goal | Metric |
|---|---|
| Users retain more after studying | Self-reported retention score improvement over 4 weeks |
| Users study more efficiently | Average study session length decreases while scores improve |
| Users return consistently | 7-day and 30-day retention rate |
| Users replace passive habits | % of sessions using active recall vs. passive review |

---

## Out of Scope (v1)

- Social/collaborative features
- Gamification (leaderboards, badges)
- Third-party integrations (Notion, Google Drive)
- Offline mode
- Native mobile app (web first)
